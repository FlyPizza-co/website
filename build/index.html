For those who never want to fly the virtual skies hungry, FlyPizza.

FlyPizza is the first Microsoft Flight Simulator virtual airline, fueled by pizza. Founded in early 2020 by a group of aviation enthusiasts and pizza fanatics alike, we strive to deliver excellence in every flight and every slice.

Fly with us Learn More

Where Pizza Takes Flight

With custom liveries for ten (and counting!) default and add-on planes, you will never have to worry about limiting what routes to fly! We have liveries for every class of plane, and each livery is unique--custom designed to work for that specific plane.

Our Features

Check out our list of some of the awesome features of our virtual airline below.

Flight recording system

We have a custom flight recording software that allows you to track and share your flights.

Events

With frequent events, you can fly with other likeminded pilots and have fun being engaged with our community.

Pizza!

We're the only virtual airline centered around pizza. We take pizza very seriously, and are willing to share recipes!

Large fleet

With ten aircraft in our fleet, you don't have to worry about which route to fly. Each livery is hand-crafted for each plane!

Friendly Community

We're a group of fun loving aviation and tech enthusiasts, passionate about what we do and sharing that passion with the world.

Custom Discord bot

We have a Discord bot to help provide METARs and charts for your flight! It also is integrated into our flight system.
